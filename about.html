Let's Build Worlds Together

I am an experience designer focusing on UX, Game Flow, UI, and Product design. As an UX designer I ensure that designs are simplistic, practical, yet highly effective. Building and testing through iteration gives a thorough and solid understanding and engages the user. From start to finish, I use my skills in wireframing, persona modeling, user flow design, and use case modeling to understand, design, and create engagement with users of a system. My skills extend to game content design skills that include but are not limited to ideation of levels, placement of events, using existing tools to create quests, maps, and activities, and prototyping through 3d modeling in Blender/Maya and testing in Unity/Unreal Engine.

Personally I am a nice person to be around and like to help when I can. A curious individual about anything and love to deconstruct to refine and create items or ideas. I always challenge the notion of "there's no alternative" since everything in life there is always an alternative if not more.
